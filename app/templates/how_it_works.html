Simple Process

Adopt in 4 Easy Steps

Our streamlined adoption process makes finding your perfect companion simple, transparent, and joyful. From browsing to bringing your pet home, we're here to guide you every step of the way.

01

🔍 Create Your Account

Sign up in seconds and tell us about yourself. It only takes a few minutes to create your profile and share your living situation.

  • ✅ Free to register
  • ✅ Secure and private
  • ✅ Takes less than 2 minutes
🔍
02

💌 Browse & Choose Your Pet

Explore our available pets using filters for species, age, size, and temperament. Each pet profile includes photos, health records, and personality information to help you find the perfect match.

  • ✅ Advanced filtering options
  • ✅ Detailed pet profiles with photos
  • ✅ Health and behavior information
  • ✅ Save favorites for later
💌
03

✅ Submit Adoption Request

Found your perfect match? Submit an adoption request with details about why you'd be a great pet parent. Our team reviews each application carefully to ensure the best outcome for both pet and family.

  • ✅ Personal and household information
  • ✅ Pet care experience
  • ✅ Living situation details
  • ✅ Why you want this specific pet
✅
04

🏠 Welcome Your New Pet Home

Once approved, we'll arrange a meet-and-greet and help you prepare for bringing your new family member home. We provide ongoing support to ensure a smooth transition for everyone.

  • ✅ Meet-and-greet with the pet
  • ✅ Home preparation guidance
  • ✅ Adoption paperwork completion
  • ✅ Take your pet home!
🏠
